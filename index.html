Who We Are

Founded by a group of passionate students, our Photography Society is a creative hub where beginners and experienced photographers come together to explore the art of image-making.

We host workshops, photo walks, editing sessions, and competitions throughout the year — giving every member a chance to develop their eye, technical skill, and unique voice.
