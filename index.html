Our story

Over its 78 years of distinction, Ananda College Photographic Art Society has created many reputed personalities in the field of photography.

During its past, Ananda College Photographic Art Society has surpassed its limits by organizing mammoth events in school based photography.

Who we are

We are proud to state that many popular and respected professionals got their first insight into the field of photography from Ananda College Photographic Art Society.

Being unarguably the pioneer of school based photography, we will never rest at doing nothing but the best in our projects to encourage school based photography.

Our mission

Promote and enhance the aspects of photography by offering opportunities to join workshops, competitions and have guidance from pioneers in the field.

As well as facilitating young photographers to present their creations and commend on them to enhance their potential.

Promote and enhance the aspects of photography by offering opportunities to upcoming enthusiasts by allowing them to join workshops, competitions and have guidance from the pioneers in the field.

Also, we are determined to facilitate young photographers to present their talents and encourage them in enhancing their potential in the field of photography.
